Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use Tribal AI, we collect:

  • Account information: Name, email address, and password (encrypted)
  • Chat data: Messages you send and receive through the Service
  • Usage data: Number of messages, features used, and interaction patterns
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device information

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Service
  • To authenticate your account
  • To track usage limits (free plan: 50 messages/month)
  • To improve the Service and user experience
  • To detect and prevent abuse
  • To communicate with you about updates or support

3. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored securely with industry-standard encryption. Chat messages are encrypted at rest. We use MongoDB Atlas for data storage with automatic backups.

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, share, or rent your personal data to third parties. Your chat data is private and not used to train AI models.

5. AI Provider Communication

When you send a message, it is transmitted to our AI providers (Anthropic Claude / OpenAI GPT) to generate a response. These providers have their own privacy policies governing how they handle data. We do not share personally identifiable information with AI providers.

6. Cookies

We use minimal cookies necessary for authentication and session management. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

7. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active
  • Chat history: Retained for your convenience, can be deleted anytime
  • Logs: Retained for 90 days for security purposes

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export your chat history
  • Opt out of non-essential data collection

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy at any time. Material changes will be communicated via email or in-app notification.
